ABOUT ME

Hello there! My name is Avery Snyder, and I am a graphic designer who is currently a graphic design student at York College of Pennsylvania. Some of my favorite things to do include playing video games, playing the piano, drawing, and of course, making designs for things. Some of the things that I design include booklets, posters, personal logos, and website concepts. I am planning to make this website an archive of what I consider to be the best things that I have designed for the graphic design courses that I have taken at York College of Pennsylvania. So, I hope that you have fun looking at what I have designed here and that hopefully you will find at least one great piece of eye candy to view!
